The Journey…

The bulb lit up when we were cruising across the city of Coimbatore after a swig of tea. The car didn’t have a radio and hence it was filled with chit-chat. As ardent quizzers and budding quizmasters, we incessantly spoke about quizzes. Our love for quizzing made the moon bigger and the surrounding sparkle. We knew we had fallen in love and the ‘Qupid’ decided to shoot an arrow into our hearts. We took the arrow and ran a litmus test. The process was slow and we wanted to speed it up, spice it up! Hence, we created a Catalyst!

Catalyst made it possible to bring in a fast reaction. The reaction was seen in all the participants who witnessed our shows. We started a mission to work on the philosophy of ‘Aiding Thought’. We began a quest to create ‘fun and engaging quizzes’. Quizzes that would make people fall in love with the game. And guess what? We achieved astounding success.

Through our fun and engaging content, we’ve helped businesses grow! We educated the audience that the brands wanted us to, and enhanced the brand’s visibility. Do you wish to engage? Well, the contact form is not far away!
 

Our Quiz Masters

Surya Narayanan

  • Chief Trivia Officer
  • 200+ shows across India

An avid quizzer turned quizmaster, Surya has been quizzing since he was 10 years old and started hosting shows at the tender age of 16. He has hosted more than 450+ shows in more than 12 destinations across India.
The interest and passion towards quizzing lead him to start ‘Catalyst Quiz Corp’, an edutainment company providing knowledge solutions and games for schools, colleges, corporates etc.
He was selected to host the prestigious ‘Holland Quiz’ for the Netherlands Embassy in India. Apart from that, he has hosted quiz shows for Government of Tamil Nadu, Sapna Book House, Amity University, IIT Madras, Rotaract club, and so on. Apart from quizzing, he is known for his witty puns and loves collecting miniature aircraft models.

Mukilan Ayyaparaj

  • Chief Trivia Officer
  • 200+ shows across India

An IIM-A graduate, Mukilan has been quizzing since he was 15 years old and started hosting shows at the age of 19. He has hosted more than 200+ shows in more than 05 destinations across India.
The interest and passion towards quizzing lead him to start ‘Catalyst Quiz Corp’, an edutainment company providing knowledge solutions and games for schools, colleges, corporates etc.
He creates content for multiple clients and has hosted shows mostly for educational institutions including the prestigious IIM Ahmedabad. He manages client requests and creates curated content. He has also organised and hosted quiz shows in the form of employee engagement programs inside multiple companies.
Apart from quizzing, he is known for his love towards art and architecture.
